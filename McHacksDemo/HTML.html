Meet Buddy

Buddy is your personal assistant that monitors your phone usage. By collecting screen-on time data, Buddy lets you know how long you've been using your phone and when.

Set Goals

Buddy lets you set your own personal goal to reduce your phone usage. You can set the amount of phone usage time you would like to stay under.

How It Works

Buddy expresses unhappy emotions as you get close to or exceed your time goal. The lower your usage time relative to your goal, the happier Buddy gets.
